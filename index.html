Privacy Policy for CPU A

Last updated: November 17, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the CPU A app (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our application. By installing or using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Device Information (Displayed Only)

CPU A may read technical information about your device such as:

  • CPU details
  • RAM and storage information
  • Battery status
  • Device sensors
  • System version
  • Hardware specifications

This information is processed only on your device. It is not collected, stored, uploaded, or shared by us in any way.

1.2 Crash Logs and App Usage Reports (Optional)

We may collect crash logs, error reports, or app usage information only if you choose to manually send the report. This may include:

  • Crash details
  • App performance logs
  • Device model
  • Android version

This data is used solely to identify and fix bugs. No personally identifiable information is collected, and you decide whether to send this data.

2. Advertising (AdMob)

CPU A uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect data such as:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • App interactions
  • Approximate location (for regional ad targeting)

Google’s data practices are described in Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can disable personalized ads in your device’s Google Ad Settings.

3. How We Use Collected Data

We use optional crash and usage data for:

  • Fixing bugs and crashes
  • Improving app performance
  • Enhancing user experience

We do not sell your data, share it with any third party (other than Google AdMob), or track you outside the app.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any optional data you send. However, no method of data transmission is completely secure.

5. Children’s Privacy

CPU A is not specifically targeted at children, but it may be used by all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Choices

  • You may choose not to send crash logs.
  • You may disable personalized ads through Google settings.
  • You may uninstall the app at any time to stop all data processing.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top.
